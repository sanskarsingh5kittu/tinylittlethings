The Beginning

Where Every Piece
Tells a Story

Tiny Little Things started with a simple belief — that handmade art has the power to bring joy, warmth, and personality into everyday spaces. What began as a creative hobby quickly grew into a brand loved by hundreds across India.

Every piece you receive has been thoughtfully crafted by hand — shaped, twisted, and assembled with care so that it carries a little bit of our heart into your home.

We specialize in pipe cleaner artistry — creating vibrant flower pots, adorable keychains, decorative lamps, and custom pieces designed to your exact vision.

Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about delivery and our returns policy.

Shipping Information

  • Orders are processed within 2–4 business days after confirmation
  • Standard delivery time is 5–7 working days across India
  • Free shipping on orders above ₹999
  • Shipping charges may apply for orders below ₹999
  • We ship PAN India — from Kashmir to Kanyakumari!
  • All products are carefully packaged to ensure safe delivery

Returns & Exchanges

  • No returns on customized or personalized items
  • If you receive a damaged product, contact us within 24 hours of delivery
  • Please share photos of the damage via WhatsApp for quick resolution
  • We'll arrange a replacement or refund for damaged items
  • Slight variations in handmade items are natural and part of their charm
